Trip Planner Overview

The Trip Planner is a specialized platform designed to centralize all aspects of travel organization. It provides a dual-functional environment where travelers can either explore pre-configured trip suggestions or engineer their own custom itineraries from the ground up.

  • Suggested Trips: A library of curated travel plans categorized by trip type, allowing users to quickly adopt proven itineraries for adventures, relaxation, or cultural exploration.
  • Custom Trip Input: A robust planning engine for self-inputting specific trip details. This includes comprehensive data fields for:
    • Total and daily budgets
    • Full itineraries and specific locations
    • Planned activities and reservations
    • Outfit planning and style coordination
    • Food preferences and restaurant bookmarks
    • Energy level tracking for pacing the journey
  • Map Integration: A visual interface featuring map placement for all flagged locations, restaurants, and activities, providing a spatial overview of the entire journey.
  • Persistence & Security: Integrated user accounts enable travelers to securely save, manage, and revisit multiple trips across various devices.
